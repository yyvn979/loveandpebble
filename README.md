## How to Actually Use Beauty Pops

The process is stupidly simple, which is probably why it works:

Scoop one spoonful of powder into the mold. Add water to the fill line. Stir it into a paste. Pop in the handles. Freeze for 3-4 hours. Apply to your face for 5-10 minutes. Rinse with cool water.

That's it. No 12-step Korean skincare routine needed. No confusing application order. You literally can't mess this up unless you forget to freeze them.

Recommended frequency? Three times a week. Morning for a wake-up glow, night for a nourishing treatment. The pops stay good in the freezer for up to two months once mixed.

## The Philosophy Behind the Pebble

The brand name comes from a sweet story about penguins. In nature, penguins search for the perfect pebble to give their mate as a symbol of eternal love. Paul's "pebble" to Lynda was creating this skincare line.

Their core belief? Your skin already has everything it needs to be beautiful. You just need to support it, not overload it with chemicals.

They moved their entire operation to Korea for three months to shift manufacturing. They're PETA certified (vegan and cruelty-free). They use ingredients inspired by their Asian heritage. And they actually respond to customer reviews with real answers, not copy-paste marketing speak.
